Introducing GraduDate

GraduDate was designed by students at the University of Calgary. UofC is known for being a "commuter school," meaning that campus life is difficult to connect with, and it's often difficult to make new friends and meet new people.

GraduDate is built by students exclusively for students.

With its inherent verification system, we provide a safe environment for any student who wants to make friends, date, find out about campus events, or just hates modern dating apps.

The app has modes specifically for making friends or dating.

It's time for your life on campus to begin. Download GraduDate today!
